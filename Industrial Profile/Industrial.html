Tenkasi District Industrial Potential

Tenkasi District is predominantly agriculture in nature and is emerging gradually and steadily as an industrially developed District. It is mainly known for its agro-based, Coir, Saw mill, Milk Processing units, Hotels, Resorts, Bricks and Hollow blocks manufacturing, Spa center, and textiles enterprises.
